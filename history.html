The history of the club

HISTORY

The Highlands High School chess club was founded in 2023 by Flynn Cook and Warren Deaton. Since then, over 32 members have joined the host of regular tournaments it has offered.

You can join today by using Schoology group code T352-PM8G-85BWX. Don't worry if you're a beginner - everyone is welcome!

Some of our regular installments include blitz tournaments -- blitz is a time control played faster than usual -- and club championships.

WHY CHESS?

Chess is an endlessly beautiful game. With seemingly limitless chances, it is both science and art.

Former world champion Garry Kasparov once called a chess game musical in its progression - just by learning the rules, you too can become your own conductor!
